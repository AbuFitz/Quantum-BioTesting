How it works

Before your appointment

Preparation guide

Following these guidelines ensures your results are as accurate as possible. Most are straightforward; contact us if you have specific questions.

Do
  • Fast for 8–12 hours before your appointment (water is fine)
  • Drink plenty of still water to stay well hydrated
  • Continue taking all prescribed medications as normal
  • Book a morning appointment if possible to minimise fasting time
  • Arrive 5 minutes early and bring a form of photo ID
  • Tell the phlebotomist about any blood-thinning medications
Don't
  • Drink alcohol for at least 24 hours before your test
  • Eat any food or drink (other than water) during the fasting period
  • Take non-prescribed supplements or vitamins on the test day morning
  • Exercise vigorously within 24 hours before your appointment
  • Drink tea, coffee, or fruit juice during the fasting window
Important

Hormonal contraceptives and HRT

If you are taking hormonal contraceptives or hormone replacement therapy (HRT), please inform our clinical team when booking. These can affect certain hormone readings and should be noted in your report.

Information

If you have a bleeding disorder

Please notify us at the time of booking if you have a diagnosed bleeding disorder, are on anticoagulants (e.g. warfarin, apixaban), or have had recent surgery. We will advise on specific precautions.
