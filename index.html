Before Your Appointment

How to prepare.

Most markers require no fasting. A few metabolic and lipid markers give more accurate readings with an overnight fast — your confirmation email will specify which apply to you.

Fasting (If Required)

If your appointment requires fasting, we ask for 10–12 hours of no food or drink (water is fine) before your blood draw. Your confirmation will make this clear.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water in the 24 hours before your appointment. Well-hydrated veins make the draw easier and more comfortable, and hydration can affect certain markers.

Medications & Supplements

Continue taking all prescribed medications as normal. Where possible, take vitamin or mineral supplements after your blood draw to avoid influencing the relevant marker readings.

Exercise

Avoid intense physical exercise in the 24 hours before your appointment. Strenuous exercise transiently elevates inflammatory markers, creatine kinase, and certain enzyme levels.

Note: If you have any specific medical conditions or are unsure about preparation, contact us before your appointment. Our clinical team will advise you directly.
