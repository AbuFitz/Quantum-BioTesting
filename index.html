Before Your Test

Preparation guide

A few simple steps ensure the highest-quality sample and the most accurate results.

Please do
  • Drink at least 500ml of water before your appointment
  • Fast for 10–12 hours if a fasting glucose or lipid profile is required (we'll confirm in your booking email)
  • Wear loose-fitting clothing for easy phlebotomy access
  • Bring your booking confirmation and photo ID
  • Continue all regular prescribed medications unless your GP advises otherwise
  • Arrive 5 minutes before your scheduled time
Please avoid
  • Heavy exercise for 24 hours before the test
  • Alcohol for 48 hours before the test
  • High-fat meals on the day prior if a fasting lipid panel is included
  • Biotin (vitamin B7) supplements for 72 hours before testing
  • Smoking within 2 hours of your appointment
  • Visiting the clinic if you feel unwell — contact us to reschedule

Unsure whether to fast? Your booking confirmation email will specify exactly which conditions apply to your individual panel. If you have medication concerns, discuss with your prescribing GP before your appointment. You can also contact our clinical team on 0123 456 7890.
