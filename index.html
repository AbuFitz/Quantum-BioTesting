Before Your Appointment

How to
Prepare

Following these simple guidelines ensures the most accurate, reliable results possible.

Fast 8–12 Hours

Do not eat or drink anything other than water for 8–12 hours before your appointment. This ensures accurate fasting glucose, cholesterol, and hormonal readings.

Stay Well Hydrated

Drink plenty of still water before your appointment. Good hydration makes veins more accessible for blood drawing and improves sample quality.

Avoid Alcohol 24 Hours Before

Abstain from alcohol for at least 24 hours prior to your test. Alcohol significantly affects liver enzymes, inflammation markers, and blood glucose levels.

Continue Medications

Unless specifically advised otherwise by your GP or our clinical team, take all regular medications as normal. Do not stop prescribed medication without guidance.
