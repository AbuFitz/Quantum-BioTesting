Before Your Appointment

How to prepare
for your test.

Following these simple steps ensures the most accurate results possible and the most comfortable experience at your clinic appointment.

01

Fasting — 8 to 12 Hours Required

You must fast for 8 to 12 hours before your appointment. This means no food or caloric drinks during this period — water is fine and actively encouraged. Fasting is essential for the accuracy of metabolic, lipid, and blood glucose markers. Morning appointment slots work well for most patients.

02

Be Well Hydrated on the Day

Drink plenty of water in the 24 hours before your appointment and on the morning of your test. Good hydration makes veins easier to access, making the blood draw quicker and more comfortable. It also ensures the accuracy of certain markers in your results panel.

03

Continue All Medications

Continue taking all prescribed medications as normal unless your doctor has specifically advised otherwise. Where possible, take vitamin and mineral supplements after your blood draw rather than before, to avoid influencing the readings of relevant markers in your results.

04

Avoid Strenuous Exercise

Avoid intense or strenuous physical exercise in the 24 hours before your appointment. Heavy exercise can temporarily elevate inflammatory markers, creatine kinase, and certain enzyme levels, which may affect the clinical interpretation of your results.

Important: If you have specific medical conditions, take regular medication, or are unsure about any aspect of preparation, please contact our team before your appointment. We will advise you personally to ensure the most accurate results possible.
