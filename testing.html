Hormones

A full hormone panel revealing imbalances that affect energy, mood, weight, libido, fertility, and long-term health. Includes sex hormones, thyroid function, and adrenal markers.

Testosterone (Total & Free) Oestradiol (E2) Progesterone LH / FSH SHBG DHEA-S TSH Free T3 / T4 Cortisol Prolactin IGF-1

Vitamins & Minerals

Micronutrient deficiencies are among the most overlooked drivers of fatigue, poor immunity, and chronic disease. We test all major vitamins and minerals comprehensively.

Vitamin D (25-OH) Vitamin B12 Folate Vitamin B6 Ferritin Serum Iron TIBC Magnesium Zinc Selenium Copper

Inflammation Markers

Chronic, low-grade inflammation is now understood to be a root driver of everything from cardiovascular disease to autoimmunity. We identify it early — before symptoms emerge.

CRP (High-Sensitivity) ESR IL-6 TNF-α Fibrinogen Homocysteine White Blood Cell Differential

Organ Function — Liver

A comprehensive liver function panel that assesses hepatic health, detoxification capacity, and identifies early signs of fatty liver, inflammation, or damage before symptoms arise.

ALT AST GGT ALP Bilirubin (Total & Direct) Total Protein Albumin Globulin

Organ Function — Kidney

Kidney function is often silently compromised for years before clinical symptoms appear. Our comprehensive renal panel provides early detection through sensitive filtration markers.

eGFR Creatinine Urea Uric Acid Cystatin C Sodium / Potassium Chloride / Bicarbonate

Cardiovascular Health

The most comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessment available — going far beyond standard cholesterol checks to assess particle size, arterial inflammation, and metabolic risk factors.

Total Cholesterol LDL (Calculated & Direct) HDL Triglycerides Non-HDL Cholesterol Apolipoprotein B Lipoprotein(a) hsCRP Blood Pressure Markers

Full Blood Count

A detailed analysis of blood cells — identifying anaemia, infection, immune disorders, and haematological abnormalities. The foundation of any thorough health assessment.

Haemoglobin Red Blood Cell Count Haematocrit MCV / MCH / MCHC White Blood Cells Neutrophils / Lymphocytes Platelets

Diabetes & Metabolic Health

Pre-diabetes affects millions in the UK who are completely unaware. We identify insulin resistance, impaired glucose tolerance, and metabolic syndrome before they cause harm.

HbA1c Fasting Glucose Fasting Insulin HOMA-IR C-Peptide

Bone & Musculoskeletal Health

Osteoporosis and bone density loss is a silent epidemic, particularly in women post-40. Our panel identifies the earliest metabolic signals of bone turnover and deficit.

Calcium (Total & Ionised) Phosphate Alkaline Phosphatase PTH (Parathyroid Hormone) Vitamin K2
