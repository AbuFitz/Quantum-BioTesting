Cardiac & Lipids

Advanced cardiovascular risk assessment goes well beyond a standard cholesterol check. We measure particle size, density, and the inflammatory markers associated with arterial plaque — giving a true picture of cardiac risk.

Total Cholesterol LDL Cholesterol HDL Cholesterol Triglycerides LDL Particle Size ApoB ApoA-1 Lp(a) Non-HDL Cholesterol Cholesterol : HDL Ratio hs-CRP (cardiac) Homocysteine NT-proBNP Troponin I Fibrinogen D-Dimer Ox-LDL VLDL Cholesterol Total : HDL Ratio sdLDL-C

Gold markers are advanced indicators not typically included in standard NHS lipid panels. Lp(a) and ApoB, in particular, are strong independent predictors of cardiovascular events.

Thyroid Function

A full thyroid panel that goes far beyond the standard TSH-only check. We measure active hormone fractions, conversion efficiency, and both types of thyroid antibodies — essential for subclinical and autoimmune thyroid assessment.

TSH Free T4 Free T3 Reverse T3 (rT3) T3 : rT3 Ratio Total T4 Total T3 TPO Antibodies (Anti-TPO) Thyroglobulin Antibodies (Anti-Tg) Thyroglobulin TBG (Thyroxine-Binding Globulin)

Subclinical thyroid disease can persist for years before TSH shifts outside normal range. Measuring Free T3, rT3, and antibodies gives a complete picture that a routine GP check cannot.
