Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 GDPR compliant ICO registered

Quantum BioTesting Ltd ("we", "us", "our") takes the privacy and security of your personal data seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and services. As a healthcare provider, we are committed to compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and the common law duty of confidentiality.

1. Who We Are

Quantum BioTesting Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. We are registered with the Care Quality Commission (CQC) as a healthcare provider and registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) as a data controller.

Data Controller: Quantum BioTesting Ltd

Registered address: [Registered Office Address], England

Data Protection contact: dpo@quantumbiotesting.co.uk

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Full name, date of birth, and biological sex
  • Contact details: email address, telephone number, postal address
  • Medical information: current medications, health concerns, relevant medical history
  • Appointment preferences: preferred date, location, time
  • Communications you send us (email, phone, WhatsApp)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we do not store card data)

2.2 Special category data

Blood test results and health information constitute special category personal data under UK GDPR. We process this data only with your explicit consent and to provide the medical services you have requested.

2.3 Technical and metadata

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URL (collected via analytics)
  • Cookies (see Section 9)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service delivery: To book and administer your appointment, arrange phlebotomy, and process your laboratory results
  • Clinical purposes: To ensure your results are reviewed and interpreted by our physician team
  • Communication: To send you appointment confirmations, preparation guides, and result notifications
  • Legal obligations: To comply with CQC registration requirements, NHS data standards, and safeguarding duties
  • Service improvement: Anonymised, aggregated data to improve our clinical offering
  • Marketing (with consent only): To send you health news and service updates if you have opted in

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance: To fulfil the testing and consultation service you have purchased
  • Explicit consent: For processing special category health data and for marketing communications
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required to retain records by law (CQC, MHRA)
  • Legitimate interests: For internal analytics, fraud prevention, and service security

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Laboratory partners: Accredited laboratories that process your blood samples. All partners are bound by data processing agreements and ISO 15189 standards.
  • Physician consultants: GMC-registered physicians who review and sign off results. Bound by medical confidentiality obligations.
  • Payment processor: Stripe Inc. for secure payment processing. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant.
  • With your GP (if requested): We can share results summaries with your NHS GP on request.
  • Legal requirements: Where required by law, court order, or safeguarding obligations.
Important

We will never share identifiable health data with insurers, employers, or third-party commercial organisations without your explicit written consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain medical records for a minimum of 8 years from the date of your last appointment, in accordance with NHS and CQC guidance. After this period, records are securely destroyed. Marketing consent data is retained until you withdraw consent.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request)
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion, where legally permissible (note: clinical records cannot always be erased)
  • Restriction: Request we restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw any consent you have given at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact: dpo@quantumbiotesting.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month. You also have the right to make a complaint to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all web communications
  • Encrypted password-protected results portals
  • Access controls — only staff with a clinical reason can access your health data
  • Regular security audits and staff data protection training
  • ISO 27001-aligned information security practices at our laboratory partners

9. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. No tracking cookies are placed without your consent. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Full cookie details are available on request.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

11. International Transfers

Your data is processed within the UK and European Economic Area (EEA). Should any data transfer outside the EEA be required (e.g., via US-based software vendors), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place via UK GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified by email if you are a registered client. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
