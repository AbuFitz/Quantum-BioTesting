Privacy policy

Last updated April 2026

Quantum BioTesting Ltd takes privacy seriously and manages personal data in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data we collect

  • Contact and booking details you submit through forms.
  • Health context details required for safe appointment handling.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited.

2. Why we use your data

  • To manage appointment enquiries and clinic scheduling.
  • To deliver requested reports and service communications.
  • To maintain legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted clinical and laboratory partners only where necessary for service delivery.

4. Data retention

Records are retained only for the period required by applicable healthcare and data-protection obligations.

5. Your rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where legally applicable
  • Object to certain processing activities

6. Contact

Email dpo@quantumbiotesting.co.uk for privacy requests.
