Privacy policy

Last updated April 2026

Quantum BioTesting Ltd processes personal data in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data we collect

  • Contact and booking details submitted through forms.
  • Relevant health context required for safe appointment coordination.
  • Technical analytics data such as browser, device type, and pages viewed.

2. Why we use your data

  • To process appointment enquiries and coordinate clinic scheduling.
  • To deliver service communications and report-related updates.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing may occur with trusted clinic and lab partners where required for service delivery.

4. Data retention

Data is retained only for periods required by healthcare operations and legal obligations.

5. Your rights

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion where legally applicable.
  • Object to specific processing activities where permitted.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, contact dpo@quantumbiotesting.co.uk.
